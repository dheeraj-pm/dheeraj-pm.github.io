My Skills and Expertise

Product
Ownership

If you are looking for a reliable and experienced Product Manager to help you navigate development through the software lifecycle, look no further. With my expertise in Agile methodologies and end-to-end product management, I can help you deliver the product that your customers deserve.

Strategic
Planning

I specialize in creating and executing product roadmaps that drive growth and increase revenue. My process is rooted in data-driven decision making, and I'm dedicated to using iterative development strategies to build products that are adaptable to changing business needs.

Customer
Voice

My approach with customers centers on empathizing with their needs and building relationships to better understand opportunities for product growth. By prioritizing gathering direct feedback via user interviews, we can ensure that products are meeting customers' needs.

User
Experience

I create top-notch user experiences through strategic UX design. Focusing on usability, functionality and accessibility, I create mockups and wireframes to bring products to market quickly. I collaborate closely with designers to ensure that the products are delighting users.

Data
Analysis

I highly value using both quantitative and qualitative data analysis methods to drive product decisions. I define and closely track progress for key performance indicators (KPIs) and conduct A/B testing to ensure that product decisions will lead to continued business success.

Go-To-Market
Strategy

I've developed Go-To-Market strategies leading to successful launches by creating comprehensive documentation, marketing collateral and enablement materials. With my help, you'll be set up for a successful launch and the subsequent sales will speak for themselves.
